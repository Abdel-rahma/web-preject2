Brabus GmbH (stylized in uppercase) is a German high-performance automotive aftermarket tuning company founded in 1977 in Bottrop (Ruhr area).

Supported cars

Brabus specialises in Mercedes-Benz, Maybach and Smart vehicles, although since 2022 they have also tuned two kinds of Porsche Cars.

OVER 40 YEARS OF EXPERTISE

With our automotive know-how from over 40 years, we restore classic Mercedes-Benz cars back to their fully original condition with great attention to detail as part of our 6-star restoration.
